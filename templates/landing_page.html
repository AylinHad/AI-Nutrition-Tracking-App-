Terms of Service

This application utilises the GPT-4 API to render its estimates. TigerMunch does not seek to serve users with specific dietary restrictions that require a level of precision that we cannot provide through the GPT-4 API. The information we provide is approximate, and is intended to help users to track their daily food intake with ease, in situations where estimates are good enough.
